w1_therapy_interventions: 

simple dataset simulating the effects of CBT (cognitive behavioral therapy) intervention on stress scores measured at 4 timepoints

variables:
-- therapy: CBT = cognitive behavioral therapy, MBT = mindfulness baseline training
-- stress score: a continuous variable representing some hypothetical stress assessment
-- time: baseline, midtreatment, post, followup


w2_3_diagnosis_stress_activity: 

larger dataset simulating multiple measures of activity-related metrics in patients with different mental health diagnoses attending different clinics.

-- diagnosis: anxiety, depression, none
-- clinic: region = north, south, east, west
-- stress score: a continuous variable representing some hypothetical stress assessment
-- sleep hours: a continuous variable representing hours of sleep measured from some device
-- activity score: a continuous variable representing some hypothetical activity assessment
-- BMI
-- sex
-- year

w2_3_wellbing:

larger dataset simulating a survey of patients from local clinics measuring perceived support level, wellbeing and other variables
-- suppot_level: high medium low
-- therapy: yes or no
-- age
-- loneliness_score: a continuous variable representing loneliness measured as a composite score from some hypothetical set of questions 
-- wellbeing_score: a continuous variable representing wellbeing measured as a composite score from some hypothetical set of questions 
-- sleep_quality: rating on a discrete scale
-- sex
-- year

w4_interventions:

larger dataset simulating effects of different interventions on symptom reduction in groups of patients

-- intervention_id: each row represents a single intervention for multiple patients, which is given a unique ID
-- participant_group: teens, adults, older adults
-- intervention_type: support group, medication, CBT (cognitive behavioral therapy)
-- session_region: north south east west central
-- lead_clinician: clinician leading the intervention
-- year
-- symptom reduction: mean symptom reduction across patient group
